the lights

If you want to have a fun night, Follow the light

  • Before you enter

    Before you get into the club, you will need to choose a colored glowstick, given at the reception to wrap around your hand to indicate your intention for the night.

  • BLUE

    Blue glowsticks mean you're here to party and dance the night away. Holding a blue glowstick shows you're ready to have fun and enjoy the energetic atmosphere of the club.

  • RED

    Red glowsticks mean you're looking to mingle and socialize. Holding a red glowstick shows you're open to meeting new people and possibly interested in finding romantic or intimate connections.

  • GREEN

    Green glowsticks mean you're interested in making new friends and connecting with others. Holding a green glowstick shows you're approachable and friendly, and looking to engage in meaningful conversations with those around you.

  • HAVE FUN

The Deep BLue Sea

The Deep Blue Sea is a vodka-based cocktail with a tart twist. It combines cranberry and grapefruit juices with a splash of soda water or lime juice to create a refreshing, fruity flavor. Served over ice, this cocktail is perfect for sipping on a warm day.

The High Tide Margarita

The High Tide Margarita is a tequila-based cocktail with a citrusy kick. Combining tequila, orange liqueur, and lime juice, this cocktail has a classic margarita base. A splash of blue curacao adds a pop of color and a hint of tropical sweetness. Served over ice, this cocktail is perfect for enjoying on a hot day.

The Salty Dog Sunrise

The Salty Dog Sunrise is a vodka-based cocktail with a tangy twist. Combining vodka and grapefruit juice, this cocktail has a refreshing citrus flavor. A splash of grenadine adds a hint of sweetness and a beautiful sunrise-like gradient. The glass is rimmed with salt for a classic Salty Dog touch. Served over ice, this cocktail is perfect for sipping on a warm day.

Drunken Sailor's Delight

Drunken Sailor's Delight is a spiced rum-based cocktail with a tropical flair. Combining spiced rum, pineapple juice, and orange juice, this cocktail has a sweet and fruity taste. A splash of grenadine adds a hint of tartness and a pop of color. Served over ice, this cocktail is perfect for transporting you to a sunny island paradise.

The Nautical Nip

The Nautical Nip is a bourbon-based cocktail with a zesty kick. Combining bourbon, orange liqueur, and lemon juice, this cocktail has a citrusy and slightly sweet taste. A splash of ginger ale adds a refreshing effervescence to the mix. Garnished with a lemon wheel, this cocktail is perfect for enjoying on a hot summer day.

The Seafoam Sour

The Seafoam Sour is a gin-based cocktail with a frothy finish. Combining gin, lemon juice, and simple syrup, this cocktail has a classic sour taste. Adding an egg white creates a velvety texture and a creamy foam. A splash of blue curacao adds a beautiful seafoam green color. Served in a chilled glass, this cocktail is perfect for sipping on a warm day.
